## AI Workflows Page (`/services/ai-workflows`)

### Heading
AI Workflows

### Body
We deploy AI agents into the workflows your team already runs — triage, drafting, reconciliation, monitoring — so the manual middle of every process disappears. Humans stay in the loop where it matters.

### Deployment model heading
Deployment model

### Eight-step delivery

**01 / DISCOVERY** — Workflow audit & mapping
We trace what a task really does — clicks, copies, approvals — and mark what an agent can own.

**02 / AGENTIC** — Agent design & tool-use
Scoped agents with the right tools, guardrails, and a clear definition of done.

**03 / AGENTIC** — Email & document agents
Inbox triage, contract review, summarisation, and drafting in your style.

**04 / AGENTIC** — Data & reporting agents
Scheduled extraction, reconciliation, and exception flagging across systems.

**05 / KNOWLEDGE** — RAG & internal knowledge
Grounded assistants over your docs, tickets, and SOPs — answers with citations, not vibes.

**06 / AGENTIC** — Multi-agent orchestration
Routers and supervisors that hand off across specialists. Right tool, right step.

**07 / GOVERN** — Human-in-the-loop
Approval gates, audit trails, and rollback. The agent acts; you stay accountable.

**08 / LIVE** — Evaluation & iteration
Eval suites, drift monitoring, and a steady pace of small improvements after go-live.
